Notes

  • The graph of $f(x + k)$ is the graph $f(x)$ shifted left $k$ units
  • The graph of $f(x - k)$ is the graph of $f(x)$ shifted right k units
  • The graph of $f(x) + k$ is the graph of $f(x)$ shifted upward k units
  • The graph of $f(x) - k$ is the graph of $f(x)$ shifted downward k units
